Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

Koffo.shop collects essential information required to process and fulfill your requests. This may include your name, billing and shipping address, email address, and contact number. We collect this information when you place an order, purchase products or gift vouchers, or subscribe to our communications.

Payment-related information is requested only at the time of checkout and is transmitted using industry-standard encryption to ensure maximum security. Koffo.shop does not store your complete card details.

When you browse our website, we may also collect certain non-personal information through cookies or similar tracking technologies. This includes data such as your IP address, browser type, and browsing activity on our site. This information is used only to understand user behavior and improve our services. Cookies are never used to store sensitive information such as passwords or credit/debit card details.

Purpose of Data Collection

The information collected is primarily used for order processing, delivery, and customer support. For example, we may contact you regarding order confirmation, shipping updates, or to clarify any issues related to your purchase.

Additionally, this information helps us enhance our website functionality, improve user experience, and optimize our products and services.

Data Security

Koffo.shop follows strict security practices to protect your information. All transactions are secured using SSL (Secure Socket Layer) encryption, which safeguards your data during transmission.

We take reasonable and up-to-date technical and organizational measures to protect customer data from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure and to maintain data accuracy.

Information Sharing

Koffo.shop values your privacy. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data is used strictly for fulfilling your orders and providing related services.
