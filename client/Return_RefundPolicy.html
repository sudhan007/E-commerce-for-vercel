Return & Refund Policy

At Koffo, customer satisfaction is our priority. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, we are here to help.

Returns

  • Products can be returned within [X] days from the date of delivery.
  • Items must be unused, unworn, unwashed, and in their original condition with all tags, packaging, and invoices intact.
  • Certain products such as personalized items, innerwear, or items marked as non-returnable are not eligible for return.
  • To initiate a return, please contact our customer support team with your order details and reason for return.

Damaged or Incorrect Products

  • If you receive a damaged, defective, or incorrect item, please notify us within 48 hours of delivery.
  • Kindly share clear images or videos of the product and packaging for verification.
  • Upon verification, we will arrange for a replacement or refund, as applicable.

Exchange Policy

At Koffo.shop, we do not offer returns or refunds. However, we provide an exchange option to ensure customer satisfaction.

Exchange Eligibility

  • Products are eligible for exchange only in case of size issues, damaged items, or wrong products received.
  • The exchange request must be raised within 3 days from the date of delivery.
  • The product must be unused, unwashed, and in its original condition, with all tags, packaging, and invoices intact.

Exchange Process

  • To request an exchange, please contact our customer support team with your order ID and reason for exchange.
  • Once the request is approved, instructions for the exchange process will be shared.
  • After receiving and verifying the returned product, we will dispatch the replacement item.

Cancellation Policy

  • Orders can be cancelled before shipment. Once shipped, cancellation is not possible.
  • In case of cancellation, refunds will be processed as per our refund policy timelines.

Shipping Charges

Shipping charges, if any, are non-refundable, unless the return is due to an error from our side.
